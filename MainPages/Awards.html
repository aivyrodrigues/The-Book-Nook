Our Awards

We're proud to have received the following awards:

  • National Library of the Year Award
    Year Received: 2022
    Significance: Recognizes excellence in library services, programs, and community engagement on a national scale.
  • Best Community Library Award
    Year Received: 2023
    Significance: Honors the library that has made the most significant impact on its local community through innovative programs and services.
  • Excellence in Literary Events Award
    Year Received: 2024
    Significance: Acknowledges the library's outstanding literary events and author engagements that promote reading and literacy.
  • Outstanding Library Program Award
    Year Received: 2021
    Significance: Commends the library for its exceptional program that addresses a specific community need or interest, such as literacy, technology, or cultural enrichment.
